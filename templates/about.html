About Us

Give & Read is an online platform designed to make NCERT books accessible to students who need them while reducing book waste. Many students struggle to afford new books every year, while others have old textbooks sitting unused at home. Give & Read bridges this gap by providing a space where people can donate, sell, or exchange their NCERT books. By giving these books a second life, we not only help students save money but also contribute to a more sustainable and eco-friendly way of learning.
